Don't worry about the bad past.
But for the future, try the best.
When the time becomes scant,
Then we don't get the time we want.
Don't live the time to span.
Before that get the task done.
At the time when they can.
Should try to finish before time began.

Failure is not a shame.
Try again the same.
Till you reach you aim.
And get good name and fame.
If you are perfect in task.
You get the result you ask.
